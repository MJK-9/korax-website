Who we are

Korax Systems ("we", "us", "our") operates koraxsystems.co.uk. We provide bespoke automation software and desktop tools for UK small businesses.

Data controller: Korax Systems
Registered address: The Merchant House, Salford, Manchester
ICO registration number: registration pending
Privacy contact: enquiries@koraxsystems.co.uk

What data we collect

We collect only the data needed to operate this website and respond to enquiries.

Contact form data: when you submit our enquiry form, we receive:

  • First name
  • Company name (optional)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Service interest (dropdown selection)
  • Your message

Analytics data (optional, with consent only): if you accept analytics cookies, Google Analytics 4 may collect anonymised usage data such as pages visited, approximate location (country/city level), device type, browser, and referral source. We do not use analytics for advertising or profiling.

Cookie consent preference: we store your Accept or Reject choice in your browser's local storage so we do not ask you on every visit.

Why we use your data and our lawful basis

Under UK GDPR, we rely on the following lawful bases:

  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)): to respond to your enquiry, assess how we can help, and follow up about your project. This is not marketing consent; we contact you only in connection with the enquiry you submitted.
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): for optional analytics cookies. Analytics does not run unless you click Accept on our cookie banner.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your enquiry details for unrelated marketing.

Third party processors

We use trusted third parties to deliver specific services. They process data only on our instructions and for the purposes described below.

  • Formspree (formspree.io): receives and forwards contact form submissions to us. Form data is transmitted to Formspree's servers when you submit the form. See Formspree's privacy policy.
  • Google Analytics 4 (Google): loaded only if you accept analytics cookies. See Google's privacy policy.
  • GitHub Pages: hosts this website. Server logs may include IP address and browser type as part of normal web hosting.

Fonts on this site are self hosted on our domain. We do not load fonts from Google Fonts or other third party font CDNs.

Cookies and local storage

This site uses minimal storage:

  • Consent preference (local storage): key: korax_cookie_consent. Stores accepted or rejected. Required to remember your cookie choice. No expiry until you clear browser data.
  • Google Analytics cookies (consent only): if you accept, GA4 may set cookies such as _ga and _ga_* to distinguish users and sessions. These are third party analytics cookies governed by Google's policies.

Strictly necessary cookies for site operation are not used beyond the consent preference above. You can change your choice by clearing site data in your browser; the cookie banner will appear again on your next visit.

Under UK PECR, non essential cookies (analytics) are blocked until you consent via our banner.

How long we keep your data

Enquiry data received via the contact form is retained for 12 months from last contact unless a longer period is required for legal, contractual, or accounting purposes.

Analytics data retention is configured in Google Analytics in line with 14 months.

Cookie consent preferences persist in local storage until you clear them.

International transfers

Formspree and Google may process data outside the UK. Where this occurs, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as UK adequacy regulations, Standard Contractual Clauses, or the provider's UK/EU data protection commitments. Contact us if you need more detail on transfers relevant to your enquiry.

Your rights under UK GDPR

If you are in the UK, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request erasure in certain circumstances
  • Restrict or object to processing in certain circumstances
  • Data portability where applicable
  • Withdraw consent for analytics at any time (clear cookies/local storage or reject when prompted)

To exercise any of these rights, email enquiries@koraxsystems.co.uk. We will respond within one month.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Children

This website and our services are aimed at businesses. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when our practices or legal requirements change. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the latest version. Material changes will be communicated where appropriate.
