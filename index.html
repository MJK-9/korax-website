About

Built around one principle.

Korax Systems was built around one principle: small businesses deserve software made for their actual problems, not enterprise leftovers.

The founder has spent his career across different industries watching the same pattern repeat: good businesses losing hours every week to tasks that should take seconds. That experience is what drives the work here.

Korax specialises in lean, focused automation: data processing, workflow integration, desktop utilities, and internal tools. No bloated features. No unnecessary complexity. Just software built to solve your specific bottleneck.

When you work with Korax Systems, you get direct access to the architect building your solution. Project scope is kept focused and honest — if a problem falls outside Korax's wheelhouse, that's discussed upfront, not discovered mid-project.

Korax maintains active support for all deployed systems. You own 100% of the code. If you need updates or enhancements within scope, Korax is here to help — but you're never locked in or dependent on the business to keep your software running.
